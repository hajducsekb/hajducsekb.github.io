About

The End of the Year Tournament will consist of 10 different sessions. All of them will give points individually with a big, combined points table at the end. The plan is to have 2 sessions per week for the next 5 weeks, with Friday and Sunday as the chosen days, as the goal is for the tournemant to end right before the Christmas holidays. Each session will be either hosted by Frost (IP: frost.rv.gl) or duc (IP: hb.rv.gl), and you can gain around 30 points in all of them.

Rules

On the events, there will be 3 kinds of tracks: Stocks, Customs and No Repo. The stocks are, obviously, the original tracks from the game (including Rooftops). They will get used during 5 events (including the endurance at Museum 1). The custom tracks will be 34 of the more popular tracks from the Re-Volt Online Pack (based on our survey). They will get used in 5 of the events (including the endurance on Sakura). The plan with the customs will be to not repeat the tracks, that way, everyone's favourites will potentially get raced. The No Repo pack is a custom pack, consisting of 18 tracks that have no AI nodes. This means that if you fall, you cannot repo!

The events will be announced on the Re-Volt Online Discord Server. Once the event is announced on Discord, you can reserve your spot for the event, which could be important in case of a full lobby!
