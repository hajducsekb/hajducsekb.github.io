Become the best runner in our Stunts Events, and get on the leaderboard! Our Stunts Events have the same courses as the Racing ones, but you have to collect all the items throughout the Ski Course. The rules are the following:

  • You drive one of our stock RCs through Skiing Paradise. (recommended: Panga)
  • You collect all the star-shaped items
  • You use a camera to record it (get a free camera for that here)
  • When you finish, you need to send us the recording, so that we can time it, and verify that you weren't cheating. If the run is legal, your name will be on the leaderboard!
